# Jewish Post-Biblical History Generation Prompt

## Complete Jewish History Narrative: 70 AD - 2024 AD

**Purpose:** Generate a comprehensive, scholarly Jewish history narrative covering the same period as the Christian history (post-Temple destruction to present day), following the identical structure and quality standards.

**Target Quality:** 9.5/10 (Enhanced level matching Christian history files)

**Output Location:** `historical-faith-narrative/content/jewish-eras/`

---

## GENERATION INSTRUCTIONS

Use the same APIs and approach as the Christian history generation:
- **Perplexity API** - Primary research and fact-finding
- **Sefaria API** - Jewish primary texts (Talmud, Midrash, Responsa, commentaries)
- **Semantic Scholar** - Academic sources
- **Internet Archive** - Historical Jewish texts
- **OpenAI** - Embeddings for RAG

For each era, generate content following this exact structure (matching the Christian history format):

### Required Sections for Each Era

1. **Overview** - Historical and political context
2. **Key Events** - Major events with detailed analysis
3. **Key Figures** - Biographical details of important rabbis, scholars, leaders
4. **Scripture/Text Usage** - How Torah, Talmud, and other texts were interpreted
5. **Christian Perspective** - How Christians viewed Jews during this period
6. **Primary Sources** - Direct quotes from Jewish texts of the era
7. **Multi-Perspective Analysis** - Orthodox, Conservative, Reform, Secular views
8. **Scholarly Debates** - Academic controversies and interpretations
9. **Legacy and Significance** - Impact on subsequent Jewish history
10. **Video Commentators & Lectures** - Modern educational resources
11. **Academic Sources** - Scholarly bibliography
